Samsung Galaxy J5

The Samsung Galaxy J5 is a great link between low-cost and high-end models from Samsung. Its biggest advantages are its compact size, high-quality camera, high-sensitivity microphone and bright color display. The disadvantages are, for example, the plastic version of the phone, you would look for metal in vain. Also, the classic fast-breaking front glass typical for Samsung phones and their Asian brothers. Anyway, I recommend buying a Samsung Galaxy J5 for everyone who wants a quality mobile at a reasonable price.
